# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary users are risk operations, compliance, product, and business stakeholders evaluating an AI-assisted risk investigation system for Skyee-style cross-border payment scenarios. They use the documentation to align on project scope, architecture boundaries, implementation modules, and customer communication.

## Product Purpose

This project defines an AI case investigation automation system that supplements the customer's existing risk operations flow. Success means the proposal clearly separates customer-existing capabilities from three delivery categories: Business AI Workbench, Agent Engineering Platform, and Case Workflow System Platform.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, operational, global. The work should feel controlled and audit-ready, while still carrying Skyee's bright payment-technology brand language.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI platform visuals, dark cyber-security palettes, decorative gradients, and diagrams where every module looks equally new. Avoid treating AI as the owner of workflow state.

## Design Principles

- Preserve customer context first: diagrams should read as extensions of the existing flow, not replacements.
- Make scope visible: every module should reveal whether it belongs to the Business AI Workbench, Agent Engineering Platform, Case Workflow System Platform, or customer-existing context.
- Keep control explicit: workflow owns routing and state; AI capabilities execute bounded investigation tasks.
- Use brand color with restraint: Skyee blue should identify our system additions without overwhelming customer-owned modules.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high-contrast diagram strokes, avoid relying on color alone where possible, and keep text legible in exported SVG and document previews.
